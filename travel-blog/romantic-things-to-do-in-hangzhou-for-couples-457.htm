Romantic Things to Do in Hangzhou for Couples

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Hangzhou, often referred to as "Heaven on Earth," is a dreamy destination for couples seeking romance, culture, and breathtaking scenery. From serene lakeside strolls to hidden tea houses and historic pagodas, this city offers endless opportunities for love to blossom. Whether you're celebrating an anniversary, planning a proposal, or simply escaping the daily grind, here are the most romantic experiences to share with your significant other in Hangzhou.

Sunset Strolls Around West Lake (Xi Hu)

No trip to Hangzhou is complete without a visit to West Lake, the crown jewel of the city. For couples, there’s nothing more magical than walking hand-in-hand along its willow-lined shores as the sun dips below the horizon.

Best Spots for Sunset Views

  • Broken Bridge (Duan Qiao): Famous for its role in the legend of the White Snake, this bridge offers stunning reflections on the water.
  • Leifeng Pagoda: Climb to the top for panoramic views of the lake bathed in golden light.
  • Su Causeway: A quieter path lined with peach blossoms in spring, perfect for a peaceful evening walk.

Pro tip: Rent a pedal boat and drift across the lake as the sky turns pink—just be sure to book in advance during peak seasons!

A Private Tea Ceremony in Longjing Village

Hangzhou is renowned for its Longjing (Dragon Well) tea, and sharing a traditional tea ceremony in the lush hills of Longjing Village is an intimate way to connect.

How to Experience It

  • Visit a Local Tea Farm: Many families open their homes for private tastings. Sip freshly brewed tea while learning about its history.
  • Hike Through Tea Plantations: Walk hand-in-hand through emerald-green terraces, stopping for photos among the rolling hills.
  • Tea House Dates: Book a table at Hupao Tea House or Zhiweiguan for a quiet, romantic setting.

For an extra touch, surprise your partner with a hand-painted tea set from Qinghefang Ancient Street.

A Nighttime Cruise on the Grand Canal

While West Lake steals the spotlight, Hangzhou’s Grand Canal offers a more secluded and equally enchanting experience. Glide under ancient stone bridges illuminated by lanterns while listening to live Pingtan (Suzhou ballad singing) performances.

Romantic Touches

  • Private Boat Hire: Opt for a small wooden boat with just the two of you and a guide.
  • Dinner Cruise: Some operators offer candlelit dinners with local delicacies like Dongpo Pork and West Lake Vinegar Fish.
  • Moonlit Walks: After the cruise, explore Xiaohe Straight Street, a charming area with cafés and boutiques.

Hidden Gardens & Secret Courtyards

Escape the crowds and discover Hangzhou’s lesser-known romantic hideaways.

Guozhuang Garden

A former private residence turned public garden, this spot features winding paths, lotus ponds, and secluded pavilions—ideal for stealing a kiss away from prying eyes.

Hangzhou Botanical Garden

Visit in spring when cherry blossoms and peonies are in full bloom. Pack a picnic and relax under the trees.

Wushan Night Market

For couples who love food, this market comes alive after dark with sizzling street food. Share skewers of stinky tofu (if you dare!) or sweet osmanthus cakes.

Luxurious Retreats & Couple’s Spas

After a day of exploring, unwind together at one of Hangzhou’s world-class spas.

Amanfayun

Nestled near Lingyin Temple, this resort offers private villas with outdoor baths surrounded by bamboo forests. Book a couple’s massage with traditional Chinese techniques.

Four Seasons Hangzhou

Overlooking West Lake, their spa features treatments infused with Longjing tea and honey. Follow it up with cocktails at their lakeside bar.

Adventurous Dates for Thrill-Seeking Couples

If your idea of romance includes adrenaline, Hangzhou won’t disappoint.

Biking Around West Lake

Rent tandem bikes and cycle the 15-kilometer loop around the lake, stopping at hidden temples and cafés.

Hiking Meijiawu to Yunqi Bamboo Path

A moderate trail leading through bamboo forests and past quiet monasteries. Pack a thermos of tea and enjoy the solitude.

Hot Air Balloon Ride

For a splurge, companies like Hangzhou Flyer offer sunrise flights over the countryside.

Proposal & Wedding Photo Spots

Hangzhou is a favorite for couples capturing their love story.

Lingyin Temple

Ancient stone carvings and towering Buddha statues create a mystical backdrop.

Xixi Wetland

Take a gondola ride through waterways draped in hanging vines—perfect for an unforgettable proposal.

China Academy of Art (Xiangshan Campus)

This avant-garde campus, designed by Wang Shu, offers striking modern architecture for artistic photos.

Seasonal Romantic Activities

Spring: Cherry Blossoms at Prince Bay Park

Walk beneath pink canopies and enjoy seasonal treats like sakura mochi.

Summer: Lotus Blooms at Quyuan Garden

The lake transforms into a sea of pink and white flowers. Arrive early to beat the crowds.

Autumn: Golden Ginkgo Trees at Five Cloud Mountain

Hike through a golden tunnel of leaves, then warm up with hot persimmon cakes.

Winter: Snow at Solitary Hill (Gu Shan)

A dusting of snow turns the pavilions into a scene from a classical painting.

From tranquil tea ceremonies to moonlit boat rides, Hangzhou is a city where love stories unfold effortlessly. Whether you’re whispering sweet nothings under willow trees or toasting with tea in a hidden courtyard, every moment here feels like a page from a fairy tale.
