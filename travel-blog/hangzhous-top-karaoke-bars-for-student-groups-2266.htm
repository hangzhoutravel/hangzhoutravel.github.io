Hangzhou’s Top Karaoke Bars for Student Groups

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

When it comes to student travel, Hangzhou is more than just West Lake and tea plantations. The city boasts a vibrant nightlife scene, and karaoke bars (or KTVs, as they’re locally known) are a must-visit for groups looking to unwind after a day of sightseeing. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, bonding with classmates, or just belting out your favorite tunes, Hangzhou’s KTV spots offer something for every budget and vibe.

Best Budget-Friendly KTV Spots for Students

1. Qianxianghui KTV (钱柜KTV)

Located near Wulin Square, Qianxianghui is a go-to for students who want quality without breaking the bank. Private rooms start at affordable rates, and the sound systems are surprisingly good for the price. They also offer student discounts on weekdays, making it a smart choice for groups.

Why Students Love It:
- Late-night happy hours (perfect for post-exam celebrations)
- Extensive English and Mandarin song selections
- Comfy seating and decent snack options

2. Haoledi KTV (好乐迪KTV)

With multiple branches across Hangzhou, Haoledi is a reliable chain known for its cleanliness and friendly staff. The Fengqi Road location is especially popular among university students.

Perks:
- Themed rooms (great for Instagram-worthy group pics)
- All-you-can-sing weekday deals
- Easy access to nearby street food stalls for a post-KTV snack run

Mid-Range KTVs with Extra Flair

3. Party World (K歌之王)

A favorite among young locals, Party World combines high-energy vibes with sleek interiors. The Hubin branch is just a short walk from West Lake, making it ideal for tourists who want to mix culture with fun.

Standout Features:
- High-tech touchscreen song selection
- Themed lighting modes for dance-worthy moments
- Combo packages with drinks and snacks included

4. Melody KTV (银乐迪KTV)

Melody strikes a balance between affordability and luxury. Their Xihu District location is a hit with student groups thanks to its spacious rooms and solid playlist updates.

Bonus Points:
- Free Wi-Fi (for those urgent social media updates)
- Bilingual staff to help with song requests
- Early-bird discounts before 7 PM

High-End KTV Experiences (Worth the Splurge)

5. INLOVE KTV (唛歌KTV)

For students willing to splurge on a special night out, INLOVE delivers a VIP experience. Think plush seating, top-tier sound systems, and even butler service at some locations.

Luxury Touches:
- Professional-grade microphones (no more screechy feedback)
- Gourmet snack platters (way beyond basic fries)
- Exclusive rooms with karaoke + gaming setups

6. Super Brand Club (台北纯K)

This Taiwanese import is legendary for its sound quality and massive song library. The Huanglong branch is a hotspot for international students.

Why It’s Unique:
- Over 100,000 songs (including the latest global hits)
- Signature milk tea served in-room
- Mood lighting that adjusts to the music’s tempo

Pro Tips for Student Groups

  • Book Ahead: Popular spots fill up fast, especially on weekends. Use apps like Meituan for discounts.
  • Off-Peak = Cheaper: Weekday afternoons often have the best deals.
  • Bring Student IDs: Many places offer unadvertised student rates.
  • Explore Combo Deals: Some KTVs partner with nearby restaurants for meal + singing packages.

From budget-friendly crooning to high-end mic sessions, Hangzhou’s KTV scene ensures every student group finds their perfect pitch. So grab your friends, warm up those vocal cords, and dive into the city’s after-dark harmonies!
