15 Best Things to Do in Hangzhou for Adventure Lovers

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Hangzhou, often celebrated for its serene West Lake and ancient tea culture, is also a hidden gem for thrill-seekers. Beyond the postcard-perfect scenery lies a playground of adrenaline-pumping activities, from mountain biking to night kayaking. Here are the 15 best adventures to fuel your wanderlust in this dynamic city.

1. Hike the Bamboo Forests of Moganshan

Why It’s a Must-Do

Just an hour from Hangzhou, Moganshan’s bamboo forests offer a surreal hiking experience. The trails wind through towering bamboo groves, with occasional waterfalls and hidden temples.

Pro Tip

Visit in autumn for cooler temps and golden foliage. The Jianhu Lake Trail is a local favorite for its panoramic views.

2. Rock Climbing at Longwu Mountain

The Thrill Factor

Longwu Mountain is Hangzhou’s premier rock-climbing spot, with routes for beginners and experts. The limestone cliffs provide excellent grip, and the views of the Qiantang River are unbeatable.

Gear Up

Local outfitters like Hangzhou Climb Hub offer guided climbs and gear rentals.

3. Kayaking on West Lake at Night

Why It’s Unique

Most tourists see West Lake by day, but paddling under the stars is magical. The city lights reflect on the water, and the crowds vanish.

Best Time to Go

Summer nights, when the lake is calm and the temperature drops.

4. Mountain Biking in Xixi Wetland

Trail Highlights

Xixi Wetland isn’t just for boat rides—its network of dirt trails is perfect for mountain biking. Expect muddy paths, wooden bridges, and occasional wildlife sightings.

Rentals & Tours

Check out Hangzhou Bike Adventures for high-quality rentals and guided rides.

5. Paragliding Over Qiandao Lake

The Ultimate Rush

Qiandao Lake (Thousand Island Lake) is one of China’s best paragliding spots. Soar above emerald-green waters and lush islands for an unforgettable perspective.

Safety First

Book with certified operators like Qiandao Sky Sports for tandem flights.

6. Zip-Lining Through Tianzhu Mountain

What to Expect

Tianzhu Mountain’s zip-line course stretches over deep valleys and dense forests. The longest line is nearly 1,000 feet—pure adrenaline!

Best for Groups

Great for team-building or a fun day out with friends.

7. Caving in Yaolin Fairyland

Underground Adventure

Yaolin’s karst caves are a labyrinth of stalactites and underground rivers. Some sections require crawling, adding to the thrill.

Guided Tours Only

The caves can be tricky—always go with a guide.

8. White-Water Rafting on the Fuchun River

Rapids & Scenery

The Fuchun River offers Class III rapids, perfect for a mix of excitement and scenic floats past traditional villages.

Seasonal Activity

Best from May to September when water levels are high.

9. Camping on Mount Mogan

Why Camp Here?

Mogan’s campsites are secluded, with starry skies and crisp mountain air. Some spots even have hot springs nearby.

Pack Light

Wild camping is allowed in certain areas, but check regulations first.

10. Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP) on the Qiantang River

Urban Adventure

Paddleboarding on the Qiantang combines city views with the challenge of navigating tidal currents.

Try It at Sunset

The skyline lights up in golden hues—perfect for Instagram.

11. Trail Running in Hangzhou’s Hills

Best Routes

  • Fei Lai Feng – Steep climbs, rewarding views.
  • Beishan Road – A mix of pavement and dirt trails.

Join a Group

Local running clubs like Hangzhou Trail Runners organize weekly meetups.

12. Bungee Jumping at Hangzhou Adventure Park

The Leap of Faith

A 160-foot jump over a lake—need we say more?

For First-Timers

The staff ensures a safe (but terrifying) experience.

13. Off-Roading in Lin’an

Mud, Hills & Thrills

Lin’an’s rugged terrain is ideal for ATV and 4x4 adventures. Expect splashes, steep climbs, and lots of dirt.

Book a Tour

Operators provide vehicles and safety gear.

14. Night Cycling Around West Lake

Why It’s Special

The lake’s perimeter is beautifully lit at night, and the roads are nearly car-free.

Bike Rentals

Plenty of shops near the lake rent bikes until late.

15. Skydiving Over Hangzhou Bay

The Grand Finale

For the ultimate adrenaline fix, tandem skydiving over Hangzhou Bay offers ocean and city views from 10,000 feet.

Best Operator

Skydive Hangzhou is the most reputable provider.

Hangzhou’s adventurous side is waiting—whether you’re scaling cliffs, paddling under the stars, or free-falling from the sky. Pack your gear and dive into these unforgettable experiences!
